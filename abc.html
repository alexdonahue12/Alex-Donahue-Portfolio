Case Study

Christmas ABC's: A Typeface design, book, and poster

Christmas ABC's is a whimsical typeface design project where I created a custom display typeface inspired by Christmas ornaments and themes. Each letter of the alphabet represents a holiday-related word, such as A for Angel and B for Bells, incorporating playful illustrative details. The final deliverables include a 20-page alphabet book showcasing the typeface in use, as well as a promotional poster featuring the complete character set.

Role
Typography, layout, print
Course
Typography 1
Deliverables
26 letter custom typeface. 20-page type specimen book. Typeface poster.

Project brief

Project Brief: Design a display typeface and use it in an ABC book and promotional poster.

Design Approach: I developed a playful, ornament-inspired typeface themed around Christmas. Each letter stands for a holiday-related word (e.g., A for Angel, B for Bells) and includes whimsical illustrative details. The final deliverables include a 20-page alphabet book and a type poster showing the full set.
