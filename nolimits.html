Case Study

No Limits: A Brochure Case Study

"No Limits: Playstation Throughout the Years" is a 16-page brochure that chronicles the evolution of the Playstation console. Through a clean, modern layout and dynamic imagery, the brochure highlights key milestones, design changes, and cultural impact of each generation from the original Playstation to the latest models.

Role
Brochure, layout, print
Course
Communication Design 1
Deliverables
16-page brochure

Project brief

Project Brief: Design a 16-page brochure that effectively integrates typography, image, structure, and pacing to communicate a cohesive theme.

Design Approach: My brochure, "No Limits: Playstation Throughout the Years," focuses on visual pacing and editorial clarity. Inspired by tech catalogs and product manuals, I used a clean grid, modular type hierarchy, and full-bleed images to walk readers through the console's legacy in a dynamic, organized narrative.
